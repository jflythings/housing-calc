NACA
Best for long-term hold
No down payment required
No PMI — ever
No closing costs — paid by bank
5.25% (Priority) or 6.25% rate — below market
Rate buydown: 4 pts per 1% reduction, max 5 pts — seller can contribute
No minimum credit score
⭐ Max buying power: every dollar of savings goes to rate buydown instead of down payment. On a $250k loan, full 5-pt buydown costs ~$12,500 and saves ~$150/mo forever.
Navy Federal
Best for flexibility & speed
Homebuyers Choice: 0% down, no PMI, 1.75% funding fee (waived at 3% down)
3% down waives the 1.75% funding fee entirely
No-Refi Rate Drop: $250 flat fee drops rate 0.25% — available every 6 months
60-day rate lock + 60 days after contract = 120 days total
Rate Match Guarantee — beat any competitor or pay you $1,000
Origination fee ~$1,484 vs. national avg $2,792
⭐ At 10% down: conventional loan, PMI cancels ~month 30–35. No-Refi Rate Drop is the real long-term value — $250 to drop rate vs. full refinance costs.
Rocket ONE+
Best for low savings
1% down from buyer — Rocket grants 2% to reach 3% total
No PMI despite only 3% down
Max loan $350,000
Income must be at or below 80% of area median income
Preserves cash for rate buydown or emergency reserve
⭐ At 10% down: switch to standard Rocket conventional — competitive rates, fully online process, fast close. ONE+ only applies at 1% down.
